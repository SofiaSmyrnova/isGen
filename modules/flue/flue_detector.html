εGen flu diagnostic module

This module evaluates two critical health indicators: body temperature and oxygen saturation. Based on these parameters, the system detects possible flu-like conditions and respiratory risks.

Normal temperature 36.0 – 37.9 °C
Fever ≥ 38.0 °C
Normal oxygen saturation ≥ 95 %
Reduced saturation ≤ 94 %
Critical saturation ≤ 92 %
